Conclusion

There are 3 important intelligences that are a part of this project. Logical, Linguistic, and Visual. The most important is the Logical portion. This flowchart shows the general outline of a program designed to determine and solve derivatives. Through a series of boolean statements and conditionals, you can reach the end of the flowchart and use the method described to solve your derivative.

Another part of the project is the Visual/Spatial portion. As this is a flowchart, it has a robust way of showing the flow of logic from one statement to another. Flowcharts like these are used every day in software development to help visual learners plan and code.

The final part of the project is the Verbal/Linguistic Intelligence portion. After determining the derivatives with the conditional statements, one must follow the instructions detailed to solve for the derivative. In the flowchart there is a verbal explanation of how to solve for many of the different derivative rules.
